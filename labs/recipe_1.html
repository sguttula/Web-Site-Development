Apple Pie
Ingretients
TypesAmount
Granny Smith Apples8
Unsalted Butter½ c.
All Purpose Flour3 tbsp.
Water¼ c.
White Sugar½ c.
Brown Sugar½ c.
Directions
InputOutput
Preheat Ovento 425° F(220° C)
Melt ButterIn saucepan
Stir flourTo form a paste
Add water, white sugar & brown sugarWait till boil
Reduce temperatureLet it sim
Put bottom crustInside pan
Fill the pan with apples and cover with lattice work crust
Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust
Pour liquid slowlySo doesn't run off
Reduce preheated temperatureto 325° F(175° C)
Bake for 15 min. in preheated ovenUntil apples are soft
Continue baking for 35 to 45 min.
